A glimpse through my life's viewfinder

Hey! You found my hiding spot!

Just kidding, this is just a little spot where I tell you about myself. To start, my name is Chris and I love photography! To me, photography is a way to freeze time; one frame at a time. Every photo, whether bad or good, has a story that varys from person to person.

This portfolio is just a simple page for me to display a bit of my work. It's still under construction while I figure out how I want my site layout. I often tend to scrap my sites and change everything!

"There are always two people in every picture: the photographer and the viewer."
